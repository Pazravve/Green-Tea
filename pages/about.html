About us

Our mission.

Green&tea's mission is that we think that our planet needs more green: plants, flowers, bushes... We need a twist in our story, so our objective is plant (with people's help) in any corner of the world.

Our vision.

Our objctive is going to make a better planet, plant everywhere, inform about our planet and about the harm we do to it every day. With little acts we can do huge things.
